Electronics & Control

Understanding how hardware works at the component level is essential for building reliable systems. These courses provided a foundation in circuit theory, sensors and control systems.

  • ESYST‑101 Basic Electronics: Covered resistors, capacitors, diodes, transistors and integrated circuits. Labs included building simple analog circuits and practicing soldering techniques.
  • ESYST‑112 Industrial Robotics: Explored the fundamentals of robot kinematics, sensors, end effectors and programming. Built and tested small robotic arm exercises using sensor feedback and motion control.
